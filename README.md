# Commodity Price Monitoring & Alerting Bot

A Telegram-based automation case study for monitoring Brent oil price movements, sending structured alerts, and supporting procurement, operations, and commercial decision-making.

## Business Context

Commodity price volatility can directly affect procurement decisions, supplier quotations, contract timing, logistics costs, commercial planning, and margin control.

For companies working with industrial products, energy-related equipment, MRO supplies, or logistics-heavy operations, market movements in oil and other commodities can create cost risks and require faster visibility.

This project demonstrates how a lightweight monitoring and alerting bot can help procurement, operations, and commercial teams stay informed about important market changes and react faster.

## Project Goal

The goal of this project was to design a practical Telegram-based monitoring system that can:

* Track Brent oil price movements
* Send structured notifications
* Provide regular market status updates
* Highlight important changes
* Support human decision-making
* Keep operational users informed without requiring them to constantly monitor market data manually

## Key Features

* Brent oil price monitoring
* Telegram notifications
* Intraday status updates
* Price movement alerts
* Structured event logging
* Basic reporting
* Configurable alert logic
* Human-in-the-loop decision support
* Extendable architecture for other commodities, FX rates, supplier price indicators, or logistics cost signals

## Example Use Cases

### Procurement

Procurement teams can use commodity price alerts to understand when market movements may affect supplier quotations, purchasing timing, or negotiation strategy.

### Supply Chain Management

Supply chain managers can monitor external market signals that may influence logistics costs, supplier behavior, inventory planning, or contract discussions.

### Commercial Operations

Commercial teams can use structured market updates to support pricing discussions, customer communication, and margin awareness.

### Management Reporting

The system can provide daily or intraday summaries that help managers understand relevant market movements without manually checking multiple sources.

## Workflow

1. The system monitors Brent oil price data.
2. Price movements are evaluated against predefined conditions.
3. Relevant events are logged in a structured format.
4. Telegram notifications are sent when important changes are detected.
5. Users receive concise updates and can use them for procurement, commercial, or operational decisions.
