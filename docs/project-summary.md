# Project Summary

## Commodity Price Monitoring & Alerting Bot

This project is a business automation case study focused on commodity price monitoring, Telegram alerts, reporting, and decision support for procurement, operations, supply chain, and commercial teams.

## Short Description

The Commodity Price Monitoring & Alerting Bot is a lightweight automation concept designed to monitor Brent oil price movements, classify relevant events, send structured Telegram notifications, and generate business-friendly summaries.

The project demonstrates how external market data can be converted into practical business alerts for teams that need faster visibility into commodity-driven risks.

## Business Problem

Procurement, operations, and commercial teams often need to monitor external market signals, but this process is frequently manual, fragmented, and inconsistent.

Commodity price movements can affect:

* Supplier quotations
* Procurement timing
* Logistics cost awareness
* Commercial pricing
* Contract discussions
* Margin control
* Management reporting

Without structured monitoring, teams may react too late or miss important market movements.

## Proposed Solution

The solution is a Telegram-based monitoring and alerting workflow that can:

* Track Brent oil price movements
* Evaluate changes against predefined alert rules
* Classify relevant events
* Log events in a structured format
* Send concise Telegram notifications
* Generate daily or intraday summaries
* Support human decision-making

## Target Users

This type of workflow can be useful for:

* Procurement managers
* Supply chain managers
* Operations managers
* Commercial managers
* Business development managers
* Logistics coordinators
* Management teams

## Key Capabilities

* Commodity price monitoring
* Telegram alerts
* Structured event logging
* Daily summaries
* Business-friendly notification format
* Human-in-the-loop decision support
* Optional AI-assisted summaries
* Extendable workflow for other market signals

## AI and Automation Angle

AI can be used as an optional support layer to summarize market movements, classify business relevance, prepare management notes, or generate procurement review comments.

The system is not designed to replace business judgment. It supports decision-makers by providing structured information faster.

## Business Value

The main value of this project is practical operational visibility.

It can help teams:

* Reduce manual monitoring
* Improve reaction time
* Create structured alert history
* Improve management visibility
* Support procurement and commercial review
* Make external market signals easier to understand
* Improve communication between procurement, operations, and management

## Positioning

This project should be positioned as:

**Business workflow automation for commodity price monitoring, alerts, reporting, and decision support.**

It should not be positioned as a trading product, financial advice tool, or investment recommendation system.
